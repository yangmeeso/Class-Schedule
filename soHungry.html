W.A.S.H.



Project Description

Have you ever found yourself not knowing what to cook because all your fridge has are random ingredients? Have no fear, I’m So Hungry is soon to be here. This application will allow the user(s) to submit a list of ingredients, they have in their possession, and the application will populate a list of recipes for said ingredients. Want to make it interesting? Try it with a group. Invite your friends to submit their ingredients along with yours and get recipes for all of you to cook together. Some of the features for this application will include firebase, multi-user function and will be mobile responsive.

How to use?
Add your name and ingredient(s) below,
and click "FEAST" button to move onto the recipe suggestions.
If you have more than one ingredient, click "ADD" button.
